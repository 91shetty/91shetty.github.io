OUR PROGRAM

WHY WE STARTED INSIDE SALES BOOTCAMP

Our Vision is to make a difference by giving our students access to great people, training, and job opportunities.

We started Inside Sales Bootcamp because we want to help others solve something that took us 7 years, 10+ companies, and 1000+ coffee meetings to figure out. We focus on training individuals skills to help launch careers, give students access to an amazing network of companies and individuals that want to give back, and have partnerships to connect great companies with great talent.

Our Co-Founder's Ryan and Mike are always available to talk with students as they consider taking a workshop.
Simply click SCHEDULE CALL and pick a time here to connect with Mike or Ryan to learn more about the program.

EVERYONE SHOULD HAVE ACCESS

No matter what economic background, we believe everyone should have access to great training, supportive ecosystem, and a committed hiring network. We screen all our candidates, and they must be approved by our team before working with companies, but we believe everyone should have access to learning and being a part of our community.

Our part-time sales workshops teach young professionals the latest methodologies, tools, and sales processes to launch their careers in tech. We are committed to increasing diversity in tech and have a dedicated portion of our enrollment for women and young professionals from underrepresented minority backgrounds.

WORK WITH THE BEST COMPANIES AND MENTORS

Our team and teachers are seasoned sales professionals that have trained over 1500+ sales reps combined.
Our mentors are from top companies such as Google, Sellpoints, Dropbox, Zenefits, Rockerbox and Glassdoor.

WE FACILIATE JOB OFFERS

After completing a workshop, students work with our partner companies in our outsourced internship program and meet recruiting partners to land their first sales job offer. We do charge for our workshops, but we have corporate sponsors that cover the cost of tickets for anyone that can't afford to participate otherwise.

Mentorship

Community and helping others is key to accelerating your career. Our Mentors are volunteers who want to give back, connect with students, and who are passionate about increasing diversity within the tech community.

We provide access to top sales mentors, and also give students the opportunity to connect with technical and startup mentors. This exposure to a variety of mentors helps diversify and accelerate each student's network while giving students a well-rounded foundation for launching a career in tech.
